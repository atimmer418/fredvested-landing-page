Overview

FREDvested LLC ("FRED," "we," "our," or "us") operates the website at fredvested.com and, in the future, the FRED mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and what rights you have over your data.

This policy currently covers our waitlist and landing page only. When the FRED application launches, a separate and more comprehensive privacy policy covering in-app data (including brokerage, bank-linking, and identity verification data) will be provided prior to account creation.

By visiting fredvested.com or joining our waitlist, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to operate our waitlist and improve our service.

Email Address

Collected when you join the waitlist. Used to send you waitlist status updates, your invitation when a spot opens, and marketing communications about FRED. By submitting your email, you consent to receive these types of electronic communications from us.

Freedom Age

The target retirement age you enter in the calculator and provide at signup. This is used in aggregate to understand our user base and to personalize your waitlist experience. It is not sold or shared individually.

Hashed IP Address

We collect your IP address and immediately convert it to an irreversible SHA-256 hash before storage. This means we never store your actual IP address — only a one-way fingerprint used solely for rate limiting and abuse prevention. It cannot be used to identify you.

Signup Timestamp

The date and time you joined the waitlist. Used to determine your waitlist position and Founder status.

Information We Do Not Collect

At this stage, we do not collect your name, phone number, payment information, government ID, Social Security Number, or any financial account data. These will only be collected in the future FRED application, governed by a separate privacy policy.

How We Use Your Information

  • check_circle Waitlist management: To track your position, determine Founder status (first 300 signups), and send you your invitation when a beta spot is available.
  • check_circle Transactional communications: To notify you of your waitlist status, your invitation, and any account-related updates. You cannot opt out of transactional emails while on the waitlist.
  • check_circle Marketing communications: With your consent, to share product updates, financial independence content, and news about FRED. You may unsubscribe at any time via the link in any marketing email.
  • check_circle Abuse prevention: Hashed IP addresses are used solely to enforce rate limits and prevent spam signups. They are never used to identify or profile individual users.
  • check_circle Aggregate analysis: Anonymized, aggregate data (e.g., average target retirement age across all signups) is used to understand our audience and improve our product. No individual is identifiable from this analysis.

Analytics

We use Plausible Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. Plausible is a privacy-first analytics tool that does not use cookies, does not collect personal data, and does not track you across websites or devices. All data is aggregated and anonymous.

Because Plausible does not use cookies or collect personally identifiable information, no cookie consent banner is required for analytics purposes. You can learn more about Plausible's data practices at plausible.io/privacy.

Third-Party Services

We work with a small number of trusted third-party providers to operate fredvested.com. Each is listed below along with the data they receive and why.

security

Cloudflare (Turnstile)

We use Cloudflare Turnstile to verify that form submissions are made by humans, not bots. Cloudflare may process your IP address and browser data as part of this verification. Cloudflare's privacy policy applies: cloudflare.com/privacypolicy.

storage

Railway (Database Hosting)

Your waitlist data is stored in a MySQL database hosted by Railway. Railway provides secure cloud infrastructure. All data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 and in transit using TLS. Railway's privacy policy applies: railway.app/legal/privacy.

Plausible Analytics

Cookie-free, privacy-first website analytics. No personal data is transmitted to or stored by Plausible. See the Analytics section above for more detail.

Future third-party sharing: We do not currently sell or share your personal information with any third parties for their own marketing or business purposes. If this changes in the future — for example, if FRED partners with a service that requires sharing certain data — we will notify you by email before any such change takes effect and provide you the opportunity to opt out.

Data Storage and Security

We take the security of your data seriously. The following measures are in place for our waitlist platform:

  • lock Encryption at rest: All data stored in our database is encrypted using AES-256 encryption.
  • lock Encryption in transit: All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using TLS (HTTPS).
  • lock IP anonymization: IP addresses are SHA-256 hashed on receipt and the raw IP is never stored.
  • lock Rate limiting: Our API enforces rate limits to prevent bulk scraping or abuse of submitted data.

When the FRED application launches, additional security measures will be in place including passkey authentication, step-up authentication for sensitive actions, and background app locking. These are described in the app's privacy policy.

Data Retention

We retain your waitlist data for as long as necessary to manage your waitlist position and communicate with you about FRED. If you request deletion of your data, we will remove your record within 30 days, subject to any legal or operational obligations that require us to retain certain information.

Your Rights

You have the following rights with respect to your personal information:

  • visibility Access: You may request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • delete Deletion: You may request that we delete your personal information. Note that deletion will remove you from the waitlist.
  • edit Correction: If your information is inaccurate, you may request that we correct it.
  • unsubscribe Opt-out of marketing: You may unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time using the unsubscribe link in any marketing email or by contacting us at help@fredvested.com.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at help@fredvested.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Minimum Age

FRED is intended for users who are 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If we become aware that a user under 18 has submitted their information, we will delete it promptly. If you believe a minor has joined our waitlist, please contact us at help@fredvested.com.

Geographic Scope

FRED is currently intended for residents of the United States only. We do not offer services to residents of the European Union, United Kingdom, or other international jurisdictions at this time. By joining the waitlist, you represent that you are a US resident.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. For minor, non-material changes, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. For material changes — such as new data collection practices or new third-party sharing — we will notify you via email at least 14 days before the change takes effect, giving you the opportunity to request deletion of your data before the new practices apply.
